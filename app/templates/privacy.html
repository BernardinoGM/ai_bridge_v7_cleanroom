Privacy Policy

How AI Bridge handles data

Public demos avoid prompt storage. Signed-in product flows keep limited request, billing, and continuity records so the service can work, support users, and prevent abuse.

What we keep

  • Email and account records needed for signup and billing
  • API key metadata and usage records needed for access control and support
  • Limited request and task records needed for continuity, billing, and reliability

What we avoid

  • Public landing demos avoid prompt storage
  • No consumer VPN is required in the production path
  • Referral rewards stay as internal credit, not cash-out balances
