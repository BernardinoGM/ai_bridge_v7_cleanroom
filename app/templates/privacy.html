Privacy Policy

AI Bridge processes account data, routed usage records, payment records, referral attribution, security events, and de-identified operational analytics so the service can run safely, bill correctly, improve routing outcomes, and protect the platform. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account details such as name, email, issued keys, referral code, and session identifiers.
  • Billing and ledger records such as top-ups, bonus credits, rewards, webhook events, and payment state.
  • Usage telemetry such as endpoint used, mode, timing, route outcome, estimated spend, and failure events.
  • Task continuity records needed to preserve multi-turn work, replay support incidents, and keep routing stable.

How we use it

  • Provide routing, continuity, usage accounting, key issuance, and balance management.
  • Detect abuse, fraud, failed webhooks, suspicious account behavior, and service degradation.
  • Generate routing outcomes, spend trends, repeated-workload insights, and de-identified analytics.
  • Improve service quality, reliability, benchmark performance, and operational decision making.

Prompt and content handling

Public landing demos avoid prompt storage beyond limited anonymous trial accounting and internal subsidy inspection. Signed-in product flows may retain prompt and response excerpts, task state, request payloads, and derived routing outcomes when needed for continuity, support, billing correctness, safety review, and service improvement. We do not publicly promise unrestricted model training on raw user content.

Operational rights and disclosures

AI Bridge may use de-identified and aggregated telemetry, routing outcomes, derived usage patterns, failure signals, abuse indicators, and operational metadata for analytics, product improvement, benchmarking, abuse prevention, compliance, and service protection. We may share necessary records with processors, payment providers, infrastructure vendors, and legal authorities where required.

Retention and security

We retain records for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, investigate abuse, and maintain accurate billing and ledger history. Access is limited by role, session controls, and operational need. No system is perfectly secure, but we use layered controls appropriate for a commercial launch service.

Your choices

You may contact us to request account assistance, key rotation, or account closure. Some operational records, payment records, ledger entries, and security logs may be retained where legally required or operationally necessary. Questions can be sent to hello@getaibridge.com.
