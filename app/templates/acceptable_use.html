Acceptable Use

AI Bridge is intended for legitimate engineering, support, product, research, and operational work. This Acceptable Use Policy explains prohibited activity, high-risk behavior, and the protections we may enforce to keep the platform safe, lawful, and commercially usable.

Sections Prohibited activity Security and abuse High-risk use Fair use Enforcement

Prohibited activity

  • Illegal content generation, fraud, harassment, deception, or abusive automation.
  • Malware, credential theft, phishing, exploitation, or attempts to compromise users, vendors, or infrastructure.
  • Using AI Bridge to evade provider rules, billing controls, or safety systems.
  • Bulk scraping, account sharing, key sharing, or usage laundering intended to avoid proper attribution or payment.

Security and abuse prevention

AI Bridge may throttle, challenge, suspend, or terminate access when abuse, fraud, policy violations, or unusual financial or operational risk are suspected. We may preserve logs, telemetry, request metadata, and ledger records for abuse review, incident response, compliance, and platform protection.

High-risk use

You remain responsible for evaluating outputs before using them in legal, medical, financial, safety-critical, employment, or other high-impact settings. AI Bridge may apply additional scrutiny to some workflows, but that does not transfer decision responsibility away from the user.

Fair use of the platform

You may not use the service to disguise harmful activity behind normal product usage, flood upstream providers, or test unsafe prompt strategies in a way that degrades the service for others. AI Bridge may limit trials, top-ups, add-on access, or routing paths during elevated platform risk or launch verification.

Reporting and enforcement

If you believe an account or workflow is violating this policy, contact hello@getaibridge.com. We may investigate reports, share necessary records with service providers or authorities, and take any action reasonably required to protect the service, our users, and our commercial operations.
