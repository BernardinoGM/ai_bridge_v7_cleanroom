Acceptable Use

AI Bridge is intended for legitimate engineering, support, product, research, and operational work. This Acceptable Use Policy explains prohibited activity and the service protections we may enforce to keep the platform safe, lawful, and commercially usable.

Prohibited activity

  • Illegal content generation, fraud, harassment, deception, or abusive automation.
  • Malware, credential theft, phishing, exploitation, or attempts to compromise users, vendors, or infrastructure.
  • Using AI Bridge to evade provider rules, billing controls, or safety systems.
  • Bulk scraping, account sharing, key sharing, or usage laundering intended to avoid proper attribution or payment.

Operational safeguards

  • We may throttle, challenge, suspend, or terminate access when abuse, fraud, or policy violations are suspected.
  • We may preserve logs, telemetry, request metadata, and ledger records for abuse review, incident response, and compliance.
  • We may limit trials, top-ups, or feature access during launch verification or elevated platform risk.

High-risk use

You remain responsible for evaluating outputs before using them in legal, medical, financial, safety-critical, employment, or other high-impact settings. AI Bridge may apply additional scrutiny to some workflows, but that does not transfer decision responsibility away from the user.

Reporting and contact

If you believe an account or workflow is violating this policy, contact hello@getaibridge.com. We may investigate reports, share necessary records with service providers or authorities, and take any action reasonably required to protect the service.
