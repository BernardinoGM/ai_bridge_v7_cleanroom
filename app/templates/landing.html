Common questions

How does AI Bridge pick the right model?
It analyzes task complexity and context length. Simple tasks use efficient models; deep reasoning or long context escalates to premium models. Quality is verified on every response.
What if the output isn't good enough?
We validate every response. If it fails quality checks, we silently escalate and retry. You only see the final passing result.
Does this work with my existing code?
Yes. Change your base_url to https://getaibridge.com/v1. Your SDK and prompts stay exactly the same.
Do you store my prompts?
No. We only log model used, token count, latency, and routing reason. Prompts are never stored.
